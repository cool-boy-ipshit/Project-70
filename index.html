Events

Sports Day
Sports Day Event In Neerja modi school

Neerja Modi School, Jaipur organizes Annual Sports Day every year, a day filled with fervour and excitement amidst thrills, shrills and cheers. The audience witness various displays of parade, aerobics and drills. The displays make way for the highly awaited athletic events where competitiveness among the students soars through the roof. Our students give their best with full energy and zeal in Races (50 m,100 m, 100m relay). The Pre Primary children also participate in engaging and innovative races like Animal Race, Hurdle Race, Sack Race, Fisherman Race etc. Not only our students, but their parents also give an impressive participation while running on the tracks and reliving their childhood moments. It is a day filled with sportsmanship, enthusiasm and memories to cherish.

School Fair
School fair Event In Neerja modi school

Neerja Modi School hosted its Annual Funfair in the School premises on December 23, 2015. It was a fun filled day in which students and teachers participated enthusiastically and whole heartedly. A variety of rides, games and food stalls were put up. The photo booth was the most popular. As the day drew to a close, the excitement reached a feverish pitch with everybody dancing away to the tune of their favourite songs. The fair culminated with a thrilling closing ceremony with everyone singing “Hum Honge Kamyab”.

activities

Sports

Games and sports play a pivotal role in one’s life. Neerja Modi School has taken special care to plan its building and layout in such a way that considerable amount of land is set apart for play which include out door sports, indoor sports and activities. The school has five fields and a 400 meter track with courts for Tennis, Basket Ball, Volleyball, Archery, Boxing and nets for Cricket and Squash. The Cricket grounds of NMS are of International standard. The Neerja Modi Cricket Academy has had the proud privilege to host the ICC Practice Matches between Team India and Team Pakistan. The dressing rooms for the players are designed in such away that it offers modern amenities to them. Swimming is a part of the school curriculum. The school’s 25 m. x 12.5 m. swimming pool is approved by the International Swimming Organization for schools.

Advanced Program

Stem Education

Neerja Modi School as the pioneer of progressive education now brings world class STEM Education to its elite students through Creya XEL: the studio based hands-on learning program in implementation across leading schools of India and abroad. Creya XEL provides an inspiring learning environment in the studio with diverse constructible sets, a structured project based learning curriculum and above all, trained teachers for delivering the program effectively.
What is STEM? There is a lot of buzz around STEM education these days in advanced countries like the United States and across some of the leading schools even in India. What exactly is STEM education?
STEM education is all about the teaching of Science, Technology, Engineering and Math as interconnected disciplines. It is an integrated approach to help students connect Math and Science learning to real world applications, ensuring they stay engaged. Technology and digital tools are an integral part of our 21st century world. Preparing our students to leverage them for learning with technological tools is key to success. The hands-on projects of STEM learning teaches children how to design and engineer, as a structured process, and create innovative solutions to problems.
STEM education has been proved to be an effective way to help students acquire skills like creativity, critical thinking, collaboration and communication skills. It does so in a way that makes learning Science and Math fun, integrating technological tools like tablets and internet usage. There is substantial evidence to show that students' academic scores improve but also students gain deeper understanding of the subject, alongside life and leadership skills.

Camp K-12

Neerja Modi School has tied up with Camp K-12 Innovations, which is a rapidly growing organization offering creative technology courses to students from Kindergarten to 12th grade to build their knowledge and personality. It has introduced the concept of App Development (a first for India) to school students; currently they have trained thousands of students in logical app development, which is also helping students perform in their academic and extra-curricular activities.
It engages students to bring out their creativity as well as motivate them to use technology to solve community and real world issues. They mentor the students to get the best out of them. Most importantly, they teach students how to communicate and grow into confident & well-learned individuals. Camp K-12 courses are designed to supplement and enhance a student’s creative learning and to prepare them to have a global edge in the use of Technology tools. To create an exquisite, fun-filled learning experience for campers, they recruit and train instructors from top engineering institutes all over India (such as IIT, NSIT, DTU etc.) who bring a unique blend of subject matter expertise and communication skills.

About Us

Infrastructure

Infrastructure

We are proud of our green, expansive 20 acre property and state of the art facilities. We have a football field, a cricket ground (International Standard), a 25 meter lap swimming pool, a 400m athletic track; as well as courts for tennis, basketball, badminton & volleyball, horse riding, cricket nets, table tennis, and squash.
The school hosts inter-school tournaments & our teams participate at the district, state & national levels in the following: chess, squash, tennis, badminton, athletics, basketball, football, archery, cricket, swimming and table tennis.
Sustainability is also upheld throughout our operations through rain-water harvesting, water recycling, and solar-powered buildings. We understand that environmental awareness and conservation is key for the future of our community and generations to come.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision and mission

Vision

We will empower students to face and excel within the ever-changing complexities of the modern world. We shall approach the education of our students through the lens of holistic development--realizing academic excellence and creating responsible citizens who embrace diversity, change, and life-long learning. We aspire to have qualified and exceptional teachers who are passionate about imparting knowledge and nurturing independent, innovative and creative thinking. We will build a community that is dynamic, multi-cultural and sustainable.

Mission

Our mission is to cultivate the leaders of tomorrow, today; to enrich each student’s academic and personal development whilst upholding values of integrity; to deepen each student's understanding of the complexities of the world; to nurture each student to reach his or her potential.
Our Objectives:
To create an environment for learning that embraces technology, innovation, and diversity--an environment that encourages intellectual inquiry and curiosity as well as collaboration;
To cultivate independent learning through exposure to diverse perspectives and opportunities;
To provide state of the art facilities, technologies, and infrastructure to elevate teaching and educational experiences;
To build high academic standards through a strong commitment to the process of learning and achieving excellence;
To nurture confidence, resilience and growth, by encouraging students to be free from fear of failure;
To inculcate values of gratitude, self-awareness and empathy, creating socially responsible global citizens who respect others and their environment;
To teach personal accountability and social responsibility by providing opportunities to constantly engage in arts, sports, community service, leadership and more.
